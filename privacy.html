Julius Discord Bot - Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 12, 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how the Julius Discord Bot ("Julius", "Kairo", "Kairo Leveling", "the Bot", "Service") collects, stores, uses, and protects information when users interact with the Service.

By using Julius (also known as Kairo or Kairo Leveling), you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Collect

To provide its functionality, Julius may collect and store certain limited data from Discord users and servers.

This may include:

  • Discord User IDs
  • Discord Server IDs
  • User experience points (XP)
  • Message count statistics
  • Voice channel activity time
  • User-uploaded content, such as images, GIFs, animations, or other media submitted through bot commands (for example profile card backgrounds)
  • Basic command usage data required for operating bot features

Julius does not intentionally collect personal information such as real names, addresses, passwords, or private messages outside of normal Discord interactions with the bot.

3. How Information Is Used

Collected data is used only for purposes related to operating the Service, including:

  • Providing leveling and activity tracking features
  • Generating leaderboards and statistics
  • Processing commands and user interactions
  • Storing and displaying uploaded media for features such as profile cards
  • Preventing abuse, spam, or misuse of the Service
  • Maintaining system stability and performance

4. User-Uploaded Content

Users may upload images, GIFs, animations, or other media through certain bot commands.

Uploaded content may be:

  • Stored by the Service
  • Processed (including resizing, compression, or format conversion)
  • Displayed in features such as profile cards or other bot-generated outputs

Uploaded content may be subject to automated moderation systems and manual review by bot staff if necessary.

5. Data Storage

Data collected by Julius is stored on servers operated by the bot developer or infrastructure providers used to run the Service.

Reasonable technical measures are used to protect stored information from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse.

However, no online service can guarantee absolute security.

6. Data Retention

Julius may retain collected data for as long as it is necessary to operate the Service.

Data may also persist after the bot is removed from a server unless a deletion request is submitted.

7. Data Deletion Requests

Users or server administrators may request deletion of stored data associated with their user or server.

Deletion requests can be submitted by contacting support at:

Email: julius [at] ericpan [dot] dev

8. Data Sharing

Julius does not sell, rent, or trade user data.

Data may only be shared in the following limited circumstances:

  • When required to comply with applicable laws or legal obligations
  • When necessary to protect the security, integrity, or safety of the Service
  • When required to investigate abuse or violations of the Terms of Service

9. Third-Party Services

Julius relies on third-party services to operate, including but not limited to:

  • The Discord platform and API
  • Hosting providers and infrastructure services
  • Payment processors for premium features
  • Automated moderation systems

These third-party services may process limited data necessary for the Service to function.

10. Children's Privacy

Julius is intended only for users who meet the minimum age required to use Discord (generally 13 years or older).

The Service does not knowingly collect personal information from children under this age.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time.

Continued use of Julius after updates to this policy constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
